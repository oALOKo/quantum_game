Quantum Squares

Master the Quantum Grid and Strategize Your Way to Victory!

About the Game

Quantum Squares is a thrilling, turn-based strategy game played on a 5x5 grid. In this game, you and your opponent take turns placing quantum particles into squares, aiming to control the grid by mastering the art of particle distribution. But be cautious—filling squares can cause collapses, leading to exciting chain reactions!

Rules & Gameplay

  • Grid Setup: The game is played on a 5x5 grid. Each square can hold up to 4 quantum particles. All squares start empty.
  • Turn Structure: On each turn, place one particle in a square you own or in any neutral square. Fill a square to 4 particles to control it!
  • Collapse Mechanism: When a square reaches 4 particles, it collapses, redistributing its particles to adjacent squares. Control shifts, but you retain the points!
  • Chain Reactions: Collapsing squares can cause neighboring squares to collapse, setting off dynamic chain reactions that shake the entire grid!
  • Game End: The game ends when a player controls 10 squares or when no valid moves are left. The player with the most points wins!
